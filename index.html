About Me!

I am a rising junior from New York, and until two years ago, I thought NY would be the only place I'd ever call home. This all changed during the summer of 2017, however, when I joined a program called PREP 9, and underwent 14 months of grueling and extensive preparation for boarding school, a form of education I was unaware of at the time. Through PREP, I received the humbling oppurtunity to receive a world-class education at one of the most prestigious boarding schools in the country, Phillips Academy. PA has been a very demanding school in terms of both academics and extracurriculars, and as crazy as this may sound, I am grateful for it, as I have pushed myself to limits I never thought I could reach and have found new pursuits I would have thought to enjoy, including a new found interest in computer science! With the help of PREP, I discovered All Star Code, a program designed to teach young men of color the fundamentals of coding and the professional skills necessary for our future careers. Throughout these past 3 years, I have gained valuable resources and experiences from PREP, PA, and ASC, and I know, with all the lessons they have taught me, I am bound to suceed!
